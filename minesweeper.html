Ultimate Minesweeper

One day I was bored so I decided to make minesweeper in Roblox. I challanged myself to do it in under 2 hours, and I ended up with a working product in that time. I did not use any outside resources, and made it all from scratch.

I used Roact for all of the UI. I used the project to enhance my knowledge in Roact and get better at all things UI. Eventually I plan to make more custimization and use it still as a way to get better at my UI programming.

It's Open Source Too!
