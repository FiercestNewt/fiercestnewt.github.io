They Have Come

I loved to play the game Reason 2 Die Awakening. It was one of my favorites. When the developers decided to stop work on it, I decided to try and recreate it. I took some inspiration from R2DA and Apocalypse Rising 2. I was never able to finish it, but I did finish a weapon system along with a custom camera system.

I had to create a custom camera system from scratch. I also had to create a custom camera system for the ots effect that I wanted, along with shoulder swapping -- though that mechaninc is not in the game right now. It ended up working pretty well, And I hope that I can make the game a reality someday.
